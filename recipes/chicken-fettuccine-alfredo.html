Chicken Fetuccine Alfredo

This meal is like a red pill in the matrix. Once you realize how easy it is to make a homemade chicken alfredo you'll never view restaurant eating the same again. That's how it was for me atleast. My culinary journey really begins with this dish. I used to look forward to the once a year trips to Olive Garden for none other than this dish, and now I'm eating it every other day at home. It is surprisingly quick and easy, and is sure to be enjoyed by all.

Ingredients

  • Fettucine Pasta
  • Parmesano Regiano
  • Kosher Salt
  • Pepper
  • Splash of Vodka
  • Parsley
  • Heavy Whipping Cream
  • Skinless, Boneless Chicken Breasts

Preparation

  1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil
  2. Submerge your Fetuccine pasta in the boiling water
  3. In large flat pan, melt .5 cups of butter and add an equivalent amount of heavy whipping cream as well as an equal part of your best Parmessano Regianno cheese.
  4. Mix and incorporate ingredients, add about 2 tbsp of Vodka and stir while incorporating sauce ingredients.
  5. Crush and mince a clove of garlic and then add it to the sauce and stir.
  6. After seasoning your chicken, grill it to 165 degrees Fahrenheit
  7. After pasta has cooked for approximately 13 minutes, remove it from pot of water and place it straight into the pan with sauce.Mix.
  8. Cut chicken into desired size portions and place into pan with pasta. Mix.
  9. Serve and garnish with fresh parsley as well as parmessan to your liking.
